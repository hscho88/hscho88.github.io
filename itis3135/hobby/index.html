BEST LOCATIONS

The best location is the mountain closest to you!

It'll be the most accessible, affordable and comfortable since there won't be an added cultural difference to the trip as a whole.

I always look at reviews, reddit, facebook, and youtube to get a sense of the mountain!

HOW

  1. Decide that you really want to go.
  2. Figure out your budget. Take advantage of any discounts out there(student, military, big groups, etc.) *Weekdays are ususally cheaper.
  3. Find your mountain. Look at the lessons they offer and equipment rentals.
  4. Get your apparel. Think of it as an investment, good apparel lasts for years and years.
    *Ask people around you if they have apparel or goggles you can borrow!
  5. Book it. Make it happen and get excited!
