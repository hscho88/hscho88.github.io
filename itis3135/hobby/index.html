HOME

A sport where you go down a mountain with your feet latched onto a board, and you try to look cool doing it.

On a more genuine note, my goal is to make snowboarding more of an accessible activity for those who are intimidated or have never got the chance to go. It can be quite overwhelming to plan and execute a whole trip, especially if you don't have someone who is there to help you out.

ABOUT ME

You can refer to me as H.C or Hazy Caribou. I've gone snowboarding a total of one time, and think I'm an expert now.

As the planner in my friend group, I definetly would've appreciated a nice little guidline to how a whole snowboarding trip goes, so I made SNOW + SNOW to be just that!

I'm sure I didn't get every single question imaginable out there, so feel free to reach out and shoot me a message. I'll try to get back to you ASAP.
