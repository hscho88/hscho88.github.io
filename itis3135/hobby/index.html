HOME

A sport where you go down a mountain with your feet latched onto a board, and you try to look cool doing it.

Despite its effortless appearance, snowboarding requires a strong sense of balance and bodily awareness. The tiniest imbalance or even change in the slope texture can throw you off, it eventually becomes second nature to you.

Snowboarding can be enjoyed as a leisure or competitive sport.

BEST LOCATIONS

The best location is the mountain closest to you! It'll be the most accessible, affordable and comfortable since there won't be an added cultural difference to the trip as a whole.

I always look at reviews, reddit, facebook, and youtube to get a sense of the mountain!

HOW

  1. Decide that you really want to go.
  2. Figure out your budget. Take advantage of any discounts out there(student, military, big groups, etc.) *Weekdays are ususally cheaper.
  3. Find your mountain. Look at the lessons they offer and equipment rentals.
  4. Get your apparel. Think of it as an investment, good apparel lasts for years and years.
    *Ask people around you if they have apparel or goggles you can borrow!
  5. Book it. Make it happen and get excited!

Wishing you the best on your snowboarding journey and stay safe out there ᨒ❄︎

WHY

Snowboarding is a super cool sport with even cooler people. Most of the people you meet while snowboarding are going to be kind, chill, and just more down to earth than the average folk. Maybe it's the energy of the mountain, the cool weather, or the hot cocoa but the culture is just top-tier.

Other than it being good for your physicality, it'll be great for developing a resiliant mindset. You fall so many times, but the best part of the sport is getting back up and doing a run just right.
