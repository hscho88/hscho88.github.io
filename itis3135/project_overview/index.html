Page Design Details

Audience: General public and potential customers.

Content: Overview of the business and homebase to navigate to other pages.

Actions: Clicking on links navigates to other sections of the site.

Purpose: To allow users to contact the business.

Actions: Submit form will send data via email to Joyce Cho.
